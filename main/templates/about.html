About Us

We are a lifestyle and performance center located in the heart of Pune city. We offer certified, high quality skilled based classes and private training programs that are tailored made to the client and corporate requirements.

Athenova Fit has a combined 10 years of experience thanks to the trainers, founders and independent instructors who call this space their fitness hub. We are a team of like-minded individuals who are passionate about fitness and mindset.

Our Vision

Our goal is always to have people leave our facility or workshop with a smile on the face.

It is important to inspire ourselves and others and of course motivate. Motivation helps us to thrive, become better, learn and be consistent. Positive energy helps us with that, transferring this energy onto others is the best gift one can do and inspiration comes along with it.

For us, the performance and happiness of our clients is the top priority. We are essentially a community that prioritize our clients throughout their fitness journey.
